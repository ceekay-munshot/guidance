Navin Fluorine International Limited — Q4 FY26 Earnings Conference Call (May 2026)

Management remarks:
Good evening everyone. FY26 revenue grew about 22% year on year, led by our cGMP (CDMO) vertical
ramping on two new molecules. Looking ahead, we are guiding to roughly 20% revenue growth in FY27,
and we are targeting an EBITDA margin of about 25% in FY27 as the high-value CDMO mix rises. Our
FY27 capex is planned at Rs 650 to 700 crore, largely for cGMP-4 completion and de-bottlenecking.

On refrigerants, spot R32 prices corrected on Chinese oversupply during the year; we expect this to
lap by the second half of FY27. Agrochem demand is bottoming but we are not yet calling a recovery.
cGMP capacity is up roughly 40% year on year and 18% quarter on quarter on the Dahej cGMP-4 block.
Net debt is at a peak this year on capex and we expect to deleverage from FY28.

Question-and-answer:
Analyst 1: Can you talk about the cGMP-4 ramp timeline? Management: Order visibility is the strongest
in the company's history; commercial supply begins in H2 FY27. Analyst 1 (follow-up): And utilisation?
Management: 18 to 24 months to full utilisation. Analyst 1 (second follow-up): Any risk to that curve?
Management: We are confident on the schedule.

Analyst 2: On the FY27 margin bridge to 25% — what gets you there? Management: Mix shift to CDMO and
the R32 headwind fading. Analyst 2 (follow-up): Is 25% conservative? Management: We are comfortable with
that guidance. Analyst 3: Any inorganic plans? Management: Nothing to announce on M&A at this stage.
